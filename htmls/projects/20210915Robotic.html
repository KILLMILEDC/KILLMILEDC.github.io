Robotic arm host computer visual monitoring system

2021-09-15 | Software | EN & CN & TC

EN

This paper describes an executable program that can run under Microsoft Windows operating system, named "Robotic arm host computer visual monitoring system" (referred to as "software" for short), and its version number is "V1.0". This software is registered by the National Copyright Administration of the People's Republic of China according to the relevant provisions in the Regulations of the People's Republic of China on the Protection of Computer Software and the Measures of the People's Republic of China on the Registration of Computer Software (Registration number "2021SR1799776"). According to the relevant provisions of the Berne Convention for the Protection of Literary and Artistic Works[1] and the Copyright Law of the People's Republic of China, the copyright holder(s) shall enjoy full copyright and all other rights to the software.[2]

The purpose of the software development is to provide a comprehensive service for the monitoring of robotic arms (or to monitor the working status of small robotic arms). The software is programmed in Java and is intended for the industry "robotic arm monitoring". The main function of the software is "to carry out information management of robotic arm upper monitoring, to complete the relevant data integration, to carry out data management of robotic arm upper monitoring, to complete robotic arm upper monitoring processing, to use the software to run automatically, to assist users in operation and processing, and to realize reasonable robotic arm upper monitoring". The software has the following technical features: "Cloud computing software, using a hybrid separation model, makes the design and system operation organic, by optimizing the processing of the data engine to complete the operation of the software."[2]

该软件是为了监控小型机械臂工作时的运动状态而开发的。软件采用Java语言编程，面向行业为“机械臂上位机监控”。该软件的主要功能有：“机械臂上位机可视化监控系统，可以对机械臂上位机监控进行信息化的管理，完成相关的数据整理和处理，进行机械臂上位机监控的数据管理，完成机械臂上位机监控处理，将自动化模式融入到软件中，协助用户操作和处理，实现合理的机械臂上位机监控模式处理。”软件具有以下技术特点：“云计算软件，采用混合分离模式，使得设计与系统运行有机的分离，通过优化数据引擎的处理，完成软件的设计和操作。”[2]
