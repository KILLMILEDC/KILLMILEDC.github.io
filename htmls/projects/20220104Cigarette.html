Cigarette gas filtration device designed based on the principle of air flow

2022-01-04 | Patent | EN & CN & TC

EN

This paper describes a patent entitled "Cigarette gas filtration device designed based on the principle of air flow" (hereinafter referred to as the "Invention (Patent)"). This invention (patent) (Patent No. "ZL202120621767.1") is registered and granted by the State Intellectual Property Office of the People's Republic of China in accordance with the relevant provisions of the Patent Law of the People's Republic of China, and the patent right is effective from March 28, 2021 and is valid for ten years. According to the provisions of the Patent Law of the People's Republic of China and other legal texts, the patentee(s) shall enjoy full patent rights and all other legal rights to the invention (patent).

The present invention (patent) describes a new cigarette gas filtering device by which the smoke released from cigarettes burned in open spaces can be efficiently filtered.

As the number of smokers continues to increase, cigarette gas pollution is becoming more and more serious, and cigarette gas accounts for an increasing proportion of the pollutants in the air environment. At present, most mainstream cigarette filters can only filter mainstream smoke, and the filtering efficiency is not high. Moreover, traditional cigarette filters are mostly disposable products, which makes the resource utilization rate not very high. The present invention (patent) provides a cigarette gas filtration device that can both efficiently filter smoke and reduce resource consumption in response to the above deficiencies.

This device is portable, easy to operate and other characteristics, has a good promotion and use value, in the promotion and application will produce good economic benefits.

CN

本文所述为一件发明创造（专利），名称为“基于空气流动原理设计的香烟气过滤装置”（以下简称“发明（专利）”）。该发明（专利）依据《中华人民共和国专利法》中的有关规定，被中华人民共和国国家知识产权局授予专利权并在专利登记簿上予以登记（专利号为“ZL202120621767.1”），专利权自授权公告之日（2022年01月04日）起生效，专利权期限为十年，自申请之日（2021年03月28日）起算。依据《中华人民共和国专利法》等法律文本的规定，专利权人（们）全体对发明（专利）享有完全的专利权和其他应享有的全部合法权利。

本发明（专利）涉及一种香烟烟气过滤装置的创新、改进，尤其是当香烟在空旷的开放场所燃烧时，本发明（专利）能对主流烟和支流烟起到很好的过滤作用。

随着烟民数量的日益增长，香烟气污染越来越严重，香烟气体在大气环境污染物中占比也越来越大。目前，市面上传统的香烟气过滤器大多只能对主流烟进行过滤，而且过滤效率并不是很高。并且，因为传统烟过滤器滤芯的不可更换性以及装置的一体性，使得很大一部分的过滤器只使用了几次就被丢弃，这不仅加速了资源的过渡消耗，而且也对环境造成了极大的破坏。本发明（专利）的目的是针对上述不足，提供一种既能高效过滤烟气，又能减少资源消耗的香烟气过滤装置。

本发明（专利）具有便携、易操作等特点，有很好的推广和实用价值，在广泛化推广应用后也会产生良好的经济效益和社会效益。
